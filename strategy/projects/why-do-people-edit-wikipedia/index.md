## Overview

This research project delves into the drivers of engagement and motivators of sustained involvement in Wikipedia. Comprising an annotated bibliography, electronic repository, literature review, and executive summary, it provides an overview of academic research on Wikipediaâ€™s editors. It highlights the insights, debates, gaps and future lines of inquiry that invigorate this relatively young but exciting field of scholarship.
